## Question 1
A dynamics glider has mass 2 kg and a net horizontal force of 4 N to the right. What is its acceleration?

Answer: `{'value': 2.0, 'unit': 'm/s^2'}`

Solution:
- Use F_net = m a.
- Solve for acceleration with a = F_net / m.
- Substitute a = 4 / 2 = 2.0 m/s^2.
