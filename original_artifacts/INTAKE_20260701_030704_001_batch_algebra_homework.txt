Algebra I homework check

Topic: Linear equations and coordinate graphing.

Students should practice solving one-step and two-step linear equations, plotting points on a coordinate plane, and identifying simple slope patterns from tables.

Practice prompts:
- Solve x + 7 = 19.
- Solve 3x = 24.
- Plot the points (0, 2), (1, 4), and (2, 6).
- Explain whether the table x: 0, 1, 2 and y: 5, 7, 9 has a constant rate of change.

Common errors to watch:
- Mixing up x- and y-coordinates.
- Applying the inverse operation to only one side of an equation.
- Using a simple average instead of checking rate of change.
