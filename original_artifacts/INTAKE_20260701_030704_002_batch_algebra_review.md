# Algebra I Review Notes

## Focus

Review linear relationships, slope from tables, and coordinate plane interpretation.

## Skills

- Identify independent and dependent variables.
- Calculate slope from two ordered pairs.
- Match a simple linear table to a graph.
- Solve linear equations using inverse operations.

## Sample Items

1. Find the slope between `(1, 3)` and `(3, 7)`.
2. Solve `2x - 5 = 11`.
3. Decide whether the points `(0, 1)`, `(1, 3)`, and `(2, 5)` form a linear pattern.

## Review Notes

Students may confuse horizontal and vertical movement when calculating slope. They may also solve only part of a two-step equation before stopping.
